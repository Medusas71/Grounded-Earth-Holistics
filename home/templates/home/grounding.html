What is Grounding?

Grounding also known as Earthing, is where you connect your bare feet or body to the Earth. Any positive ions are neutralized by the negative ions called electrons. Electrons move freely between the Earth and the grounded human body. The Earth’s electrons enters our body. When the body has an abundant amount of electrons, it improves our immune system in so many ways.

Inflammation is the biggest cause of chronic diseases. Multiple studies have shown that grounding on a daily basis, dramatically reduces this inflammation. Stimulating the small nerves in the bottom of your feet and connect to the earth’s surface will restore your body to its natural state/rhythm.

So how do you ground yourself?

Go outside, take off your shoes and walk barefoot on the grass, dirt or sand for approx 10 minutes to start with. You will notice the benefits. The grass, dirt and sand are conductive surfaces from which your body will draw the Earth’s energy. The more time you spend outside whether barefoot, or lying down on the grass, the more you will notice the benefits. Grounding calms your body releasing stress and tension. So next time you are feeling overwhelmed, stressed or have aches and pains, go outside and try it out yourself. You won’t regret it!
